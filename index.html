Zimbabwe is a landlocked country in Southern Africa known for its diverse landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and historical significance. It is home to natural wonders such as Victoria Falls, one of the largest and most famous waterfalls in the world, as well as vast national parks that support abundant wildlife. Zimbabwe has a strong cultural identity shaped by traditions, music, and art of ethnic groups like the Shona and Ndebele.
