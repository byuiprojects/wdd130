Zambezi Rafting Adventure

At RiverRush White Water Rafting, our mission is to deliver unforgettable adventures while prioritizing safety, teamwork, and respect for nature. We believe every rapid tells a story, and every trip builds confidence, courage, and lifelong memories.

History

The Zambezi River is one of Africa’s greatest rivers, flowing through six countries before reaching the Indian Ocean. The famous Zambezi rapids, located just below Victoria Falls, were formed over thousands of years as powerful water carved deep gorges into solid basalt rock. Long before rafting began, the river was vital to local communities for fishing, travel, and spiritual significance. In the late 20th century, adventurers recognized the Zambezi’s intense rapids—some of the strongest commercially rafted rapids in the world—as ideal for white water rafting. Since then, the Zambezi rapids have become a global adventure destination, attracting thrill-seekers while remaining a symbol of natural power, beauty, and resilience.
